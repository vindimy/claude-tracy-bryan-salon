The Salon

Since 2019, Tracy Bryan's Gentlemen Salon has been Westwood's destination for men who take their appearance seriously. Founded by Tracy Bryan — a seasoned hairstylist with an eye for detail and a gift for listening — the salon combines the precision of a master stylist with the comfort of a private club.

Private treatment rooms. Complimentary beverages. No rush, no noise. Just exceptional grooming.
